About

I'm a PhD student at the Programming Methods Laboratory (LAMP) at EPFL under the supervision of Professor Martin Odersky.

My main research interests are in programming languages, with a particular focus on semantics of domain-specific languages, as well as formal methods for scientific modeling.

I have also written a GNU/Emacs mode for Kappa.
